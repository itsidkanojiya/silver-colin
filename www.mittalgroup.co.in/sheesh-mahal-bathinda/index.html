Overview

Step Into The Luxurious World!

Ready to experience the flavor of an ultra-modern lifestyle that will usher the inhabitants of Bhatinda into a new era. Sheesh Mahal, an ultra-modern self-contained township with a variety of housing options and a wide range of lifestyle amenities, is supported by the greatest infrastructure.

If you are thinking of moving and buying a luxury house with all the facilities and are also focused on being responsible for our environment, Sheesh Mahal is the place to live in.

Highlights

Explore the list of Facilities Provided by Sheesh Mahal Ahmedabad
  • Spread over 43.11 acres and PUDA approved
  • Excellent Civic infrastructure
  • More than 180 living families
  • Located on Ahmedabad-Dabwali Road
  • Lush Green Lawns
  • Well Planned and well-lit metalled roads
  • Regular Canal water supply
  • No Waterlogging
  • Rain water harvesting
  • No overhead cables
  • Walled township with 24/7 security

Location

Checkout the Location advantages of Sheesh Mahal Ahmedabad.
  • Located on Ahmedabad – Dabwali Road (Ahmedabad Delhi Highway No. 54)
  • 4 minutes drive to Model Town & Civil Lines
  • 4 minutes drive to Civil Hospital
  • 4.5 Kms. drive to Ahmedabad Bus Stand
  • 6.3 Kms. drive to Ahmedabad Railway Station
  • 3.2 Kms. drive to AIIMS
  • 29 Kms. drive to Ahmedabad Airport
  • 40 Kms. drive to HPCL Refinery
  • 20 Kms. drive to Central University
  • 7.5 Kms. drive to Mittal’s City Mall
  • 2.1 Kms. drive to G.Z.S. C.E T & Maharaja Ranjit Singh Technical University
